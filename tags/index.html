QoS Aware Energy Efficient Vertical Handover Approach in LTE/WLAN Heterogeneous Networks

Seamless mobility between different radio access networks is required in the next-generation cellular and wireless heterogeneous environments to provide uninterrupted service to the user. Maintaining the Quality of Service (QoS) required per application during the handover process in between different access networks (Vertical Handover) and, optimizing the power consumption in a mobile terminal during the vertical handover process in a heterogeneous environment are quite challenging. The proposed method uses a Fuzzy Logic-based vertical handover decision algorithm which assesses multiple input parameters and determines the most desirable network preference between LTE and WLAN to perform the handover.

Exploring Base Dimension Reduction PCA Technique for Going Forward in ML

Delving into the fascinating world of clustering projects, I found myself intrigued by Principal Component Analysis (PCA), prompting me to consider it as a subject for an upcoming blog. PCA, a linear method, aims to retain a dataset’s variance while reducing its dimensionality by transforming initial features into uncorrelated primary components. This technique proves invaluable in scenarios where datasets exhibit high dimensionality, making it challenging to see underlying patterns. By standardizing the dataset and following a stepwise approach, PCA facilitates the identification of principal components, which capture the highest variance in the data.
