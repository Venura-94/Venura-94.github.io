Analyzing Total Revenue and Stock Price Trends of Tesla and GameStop

December 30, 2023

The “Extracting-and-Visualizing-Stock-Data” project aims to provide a comprehensive analysis of the stock performance and revenue trends of Tesla and GameStop. Leveraging a combination of Python libraries including BeautifulSoup for web scraping and yfinance for accessing stock data, this project delves into various aspects to offer insights into the financial performance of these companies.

Extracting Tesla Stock Data

Utilizing the yfinance library, the project extracts historical stock data of Tesla, including opening and closing prices, trading volume, and other relevant metrics.

Scraping Tesla Revenue Data

Web scraping techniques are employed to extract revenue data specifically for Tesla from reputable online sources. This includes revenue figures over time, allowing for the analysis of revenue trends and patterns.

Extracting GameStop Stock Data

Similar to the Tesla stock data extraction, this step involves using the yfinance library to fetch historical stock data for GameStop, enabling comparison and analysis alongside Tesla’s data.

Scraping GameStop Revenue Data

Through web scraping methods, revenue data for GameStop is extracted from reliable sources. This data will facilitate a comparative analysis between Tesla and GameStop in terms of revenue performance.

Visualizing Tesla Stock Graph

Utilizing the collected Tesla stock data, this step involves creating insightful visualizations such as line graphs to illustrate the historical stock price trends of Tesla over a specified period.

Visualizing GameStop Stock Graph

This task focuses on visualizing the historical stock price trends of GameStop using the extracted stock data.

By completing these tasks, the project aims to provide a comprehensive analysis of both the stock performance and revenue trends of Tesla and GameStop. The generated visualizations and insights can be valuable for investors and stakeholders seeking to understand the financial dynamics of these companies, thereby informing investment decisions.
